Stray Kids

Stray Kids are known for their self-produced, genre-blending music (often called "Mala flavor") featuring powerful hip-hop, EDM, and emotional ballads, with lyrics exploring youthful struggles.
Key elements include their in-house production team 3RACHA (Bang Chan, Changbin, Han), distinct sound, energetic performances, strong connection with fans (STAY) through extensive content, and authentic storytelling.

Debut: March 25, 2018

Genre: K-pop, hip-hop, and EDM
